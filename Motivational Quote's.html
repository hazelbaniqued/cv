Motivational Quote's



"Success is not final; failure is not fatal:
it is the courage to continue that counts."

Winston S. Churchill


"It is better to fail in originality
than to succeed in imitation."

Herman Melville


"The road to success and the road to failure
are almost exactly the same."

Colin R. Davis


"Success usually comes to those who are
too busy looking for it."

Henry David Thoreau


"Opportunities don't happen.
You create them."

Chris Grosser



These kind of words of wisdom defined how much I want to achieve my goals in life.
It motivates me to encourage myself to aim high as much as I can!
